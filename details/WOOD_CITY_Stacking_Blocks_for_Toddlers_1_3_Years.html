WOOD CITY Stacking Toy: Fun Meets Learning for Little Explorers

The WOOD CITY stacking toy is a versatile and educational play companion for toddlers aged 1 to 3. Inspired by Montessori methods, it helps build problem-solving skills, hand-eye coordination, and spatial awareness. Featuring 4 different colored and shaped blocks, this toy introduces children to geometry and color theory in an engaging way. The baseboard includes 4 turtle-shaped compartments, which allow kids to stack, sort, and reconfigure the pieces with endless creativity, blending learning and play seamlessly.
