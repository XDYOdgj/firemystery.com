Hammont Gingham Napkins

Add a touch of elegance and charm to your next gathering with these delightful Hammont Gingham Napkins. Their classic red and white checkered pattern brings a timeless, festive feel to any table, whether you're hosting a casual get-together, a backyard barbecue, or a special celebration.

Crafted from premium quality paper, these napkins are soft to the touch, strong enough for any mess, and easy to clean up. They are perfect for use in both indoor and outdoor settings, making them a versatile and reliable choice for all occasions. With their disposable design, you won't need to worry about washing or storing them, saving you time and effort while keeping your event running smoothly.

Each pack contains two different checkered designs, allowing you to mix and match for a personalized and eye-catching look. These napkins are ideal for weddings, outdoor events, and festive parties, making them an excellent addition to your event party supplies and backyard decoration.
