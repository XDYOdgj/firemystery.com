Angelbaby Reborn Baby Girl Doll

A beautifully crafted, realistic baby doll designed for both comfort and elegance. Perfect for display, play, or as a special keepsake.

Realistic Details, Perfect Touch

This Angelbaby Reborn Toddler Girl Doll is created with meticulous attention to detail, offering a hyper-realistic appearance that feels like a real baby. It stands at an impressive 24 inches tall (60 cm) and weighs about 1500 grams, making it easy to handle and snuggle with.

The head and limbs are made from high-quality silicone vinyl, providing a soft, lifelike texture that is both safe and durable. The weighted body is filled with PP cotton, adding a comforting feel and enhancing the realistic weight of the doll.

Interactive & Stylish Features

Equipped with a magnetic mouth and an included pacifier for safe interaction, this doll is perfect for kids who love to play with baby toys. Its design allows for easy attachment of the pacifier, creating a unique and fun experience.

The wig hair is hand-applied and styled with a hair iron, provided the temperature is kept under 120°C. This lets you personalize the doll’s look and create a more realistic appearance. Combine with the other accessories for a complete baby-themed item.
