Montessori Wooden Toy Set for 1 Year Old

This 5-in-1 Montessori wooden toy set is designed for babies aged 1 year old, combining fun and learning in every detail. Each toy is carefully crafted to support essential developmental milestones, helping your child grow with confidence and curiosity.

Perfect for Early Learning and Play

The toy set includes five educational pieces that work together seamlessly to foster different skills. From the Peg Drop Box that strengthens fine motor abilities to the Rocking Stacker that improves balance and logical thinking, each item is a valuable tool for toddler development.

Safe, Durable, and Eco-Friendly

All toys are made from high-quality, non-toxic wood, ensuring a safe play environment for your baby. The materials are also eco-friendly, making this set an excellent choice for parents who value sustainability and long-term use.

Encourages Sensory Exploration

The inclusion of crinkle and textured elements makes this set ideal for baby sensory learning. The Egg Cup and Crinkle Bag are particularly great for engaging touch, sound, and sight, keeping your little one entertained while they learn.

Every set includes a comprehensive parent guide that helps you support your child's development effectively. This is a great resource for families looking to create a purposeful and engaging learning environment for their little one.
